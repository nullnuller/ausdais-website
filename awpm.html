AWPM (TM) - Air and Water Pollution Monitoring

AWPM is an advanced monitoring system designed to track air and water pollution levels. By providing real-time data and comprehensive analysis, AWPM helps environmental agencies and industries manage pollution and comply with regulations.

Features:

  • Real-time pollution monitoring
  • Data visualization for air and water quality
  • Integration with various sensors
  • Customizable alerts and notifications
  • Historical data analysis and reporting
